Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2025

1. Introduction

This app ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. The app does not require login or account creation.

The only data retrieved is recipe-related content stored in our Firebase Firestore database, which consists of copyright-free meal and nutrition data.

3. How We Use Data

We use the retrieved data solely to:

  • Display meals and categories in the app.
  • Help users plan their meals through the "My Plate Planner" feature.

No user-specific analytics or tracking is used.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. All recipe content is sourced from a structured Firebase database we manage.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect or store any information from children under 13 years of age.

6. Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase Firestore, a service provided by Google, to store and retrieve non-personal content. You can read their privacy policy here:
